Add 4 nettle leaves into a white marble mortar
Add 3 dragon claws and crush these ingredients into a fine powder
Add 3 measurements of this powder into a brass cauldron
Crack an egg using a silver spoon and add the cracked egg including its sell in the cauldron
Add 2 eyes from two different newts
Bring the potion to boil and let it simmer for 23 minutes
Serve the potion in a chrystal glass
