Crush 3g of dried dandelion roots using a mortar and pestle
Pour the resulting powder into a brass cauldron
Add 23 droops of bat blood
Add 2 bettle carapaces 
Add three drops of lime juice
Boil and simmer the potion for 1 hour
Stir once every 12 minutes, anti-clockwise
Pour the potion in a golden cup and let it cool down for 7 minutes before drinking
